# MROS
Native Mobile Restaurant Ordering Software With QRCode

The design was inspired by the "Yemeksepeti" application.

## First we must be in a restaurant
## Scan QRCode
## Restaurant's Menu will be shown your mobile device
## Choose your product
## Confirm your order
## Enjoy your meal
